Frequently Asked Questions

What is a hotel management system (HMS)?

A hotel management system (HMS) is software used by hotels to control, automate, optimize, and streamline operations across departments. The core functions of an HMS include managing property-wide workflows like reservation systems, room inventory and occupancy, pricing and reporting.
